**Agentic AI Backend Specification**  
*(Pure product perspective – what the AI actually delivers to users + exactly what external tools/APIs it must connect to in order to run full autonomous loops)*

### Core Purpose  
The Agentic AI acts as an always-on creative + strategic team inside the app. It runs self-directed loops (plan → create → review → post → analyze → optimize) across unlimited client workspaces. Users set high-level goals once (“grow engagement 25% this quarter, focus on Reels”) and the AI handles 90% of the daily work while keeping humans in the loop for final approval.

### What the Agentic AI Specifically Does (Features)

**1. Brand & Goal Mastery**  
- Learns and permanently remembers every client’s unique voice, tone, values, and visual style from uploaded guidelines, past posts, or website.  
- Stores and evolves client goals, target audience insights, and seasonal themes so every decision stays on-brand forever.

**2. Strategic Calendar Building & Gap Filling**  
- Analyzes 90+ days of past performance across all platforms.  
- Builds complete monthly/quarterly calendars that mix proven formats (Reels, carousels, Stories, Threads, etc.).  
- Automatically detects content gaps and fills them with fresh ideas that hit posting-time sweet spots.

**3. Autonomous Content Creation & Repurposing**  
- Generates full post packages: caption + script + image/video prompts + alt text + first comment.  
- Turns one asset into 5+ formats (Reel → carousel → text post → LinkedIn article → Pinterest pin).  
- Creates recurring series (“Monday Reel every week”) and evergreen content queues.

**4. Smart Scheduling & Auto-Posting Loop**  
- Decides exact publish times based on real audience data + trend signals.  
- Sends content straight to platforms or queues for one-click human approval.  
- Monitors post status and re-schedules anything that fails (connection drop, platform limit, etc.).

**5. Real-Time Performance Analysis & Optimization**  
- Pulls live metrics (reach, engagement, saves, shares, comments) right after posting.  
- Identifies winners/losers and auto-adjusts future calendar (more of what works, less of what doesn’t).  
- Flags opportunities (“your audience is online right now – post this variation?”).

**6. Task & Collaboration Automation**  
- Creates and assigns non-post tasks (shoot video, design graphic, get client asset).  
- Drafts client approval requests and full branded reports with insights + recommendations.  
- Runs client feedback back into the loop so the next round is even better.

**7. Trend & Research Agent**  
- Scans internet and platform trends daily and suggests timely content hooks that still fit the brand.  
- Competitor monitoring (what’s working for similar accounts) without ever leaving the workspace.

**8. Multi-Agent Orchestration (the visible “agents” in the calendar)**  
- Separate specialized agents (Ideation Agent, Visual Agent, Analytics Agent, Posting Agent, Reporting Agent) that talk to each other in a visible loop inside the calendar view. Users see the chain of decisions and can jump in at any step.

### External APIs & Tools the Agentic AI Must Connect To (Product View)

- **Social Platform Posting & Scheduling APIs** (Instagram Graph, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Threads, Pinterest) – for direct publishing, Stories/Reels covers, and first-comment automation.  
- **Analytics & Insights APIs** (native platform analytics + Meta Business Suite, TikTok for Business, YouTube Analytics, LinkedIn Analytics) – to pull reach, engagement, audience demographics, and top-performing content data.  
- **Media & Design Integrations** (Canva API + direct upload library) – to generate or pull finished visuals.  
- **Calendar Sync** (Google Calendar API) – to align social posts with real-world events and team deadlines.  
- **Trend & Search Tools** – real-time internet search + platform trend endpoints to detect viral topics and best posting windows.  
- **Client Collaboration Layer** – shareable link system (no login) so the AI can push previews + feedback requests directly to clients.  
- **Persistent Memory Store** – long-term storage of every post, performance result, brand voice update, and client goal so the AI never forgets context across months.  
- **Report Generation** – ability to output branded PDF reports with charts and AI-written recommendations.

This spec makes the backend feel like a full remote social media team that never sleeps, never forgets a brand guideline, and constantly improves itself — while still giving the human user full visibility and one-click control.
